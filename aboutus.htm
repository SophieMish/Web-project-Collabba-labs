The wedding brand Collabba is a popular Russian manufacturer of wedding dresses, and more recently, evening dresses. Collabba became the embodiment of the ideas of two people - a professional wedding business with many years of experience Natalya Mishukova and a young and talented designer Irina Kazina. The unique style of “Collabba” is that the designer in a special way embodies all current trends, creating very feminine, light and airy dresses. Irina is a very modest, silent, fragile girl with a rich imagination and inner world. Inspired by nature in all its manifestations, this is felt in dresses, where floral and floral lace patterns are often present. The collections created by the designer fully reflect the character of the brand. The basis of the collections are A-line dresses popular among brides. The designer added her vision to each model, diversifying them with various elements:
  1. sleeves - lanterns made of lace
  2. sleeves-wings made of light tulle
  3. jewelry made of beads and pearls
In general, the Collabba collections are the embodiment of femininity and beauty, and each dress is a work of art, concentrated in it are craftsmanship and design inspiration, energy and love of the whole team.
